Security Policies

At EONpool, we prioritize the security of our staking operations and the assets of our customers. Our comprehensive security policies ensure a reliable and secure staking experience.

Here are the key security measures we adhere to:

Access Control: Access to validator nodes and sensitive information is restricted to authorized personnel only.
Encryption: We encrypt sensitive data both in transit and at rest to safeguard against unauthorized access.
Regular Security Audits: Our infrastructure undergoes regular security audits to ensure it remains secure and up-to-date.
Incident Response Plan: We have a clear incident response plan to handle security breaches effectively, with defined roles and responsibilities.
Firewall Configuration: Our validators are protected by properly configured firewalls to block unauthorized network traffic.
Monitoring and Alerting: We have systems in place to monitor and alert us to security threats, ensuring timely responses.
